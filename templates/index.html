About Us

EAST WEST MANAGEMENT CENTER

is an authorized center for Japanese Language NAT TEST management in Kathmandu. This center is responsible for conducting NAT test examination and also distributing the results of the examination including score report and certificate of proficiency. For the convenience of the students, we have been providing both online and offline services for filling up the NAT test form. However, there is no such online provision for distribution of the exam score report.

This center operates fully under Senmon Kyouiku Publishing Co. (Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo, Japan) as a regional office of Kathmandu district.
